About
Joshua has a diverse professional background, with experience in technical support, publishing, distribution logistics, events planning, international relations, nonprofits, foodservice, and youth mentorship. Feel free to check out his LinkedIn profile and resume for a more comprehensive summary. After more than two years in technical roles of increasing responsibility at a software company in Midtown Atlanta, in April 2018 Joshua formally transitioned into the exciting world of software development, embarking on a 16-week full-stack immersive course at DigitalCrafts in Buckhead. The applied, hands-on knowledge gained from the intensive program supplements his academic studies in computing at Kennesaw State University, from which he holds a graduate certificate in computer science foundations.

In his free time, Joshua enjoys learning foreign languages, watching films, jogging and hiking, eating good food, and hanging out with his kind wife and their crazy kitty.
